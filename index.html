The Chopin Library

The Chopin Library is a website aimed to archive piano classics from an assortment of well-known composers, most of whom contributed to Western music during the common practice period. The aim of this website is to provide a quick and easy way for piano students to download, view and read scores from these composers without having to navigate through the chaotic results of the search engine.

The website has been named after the romantic era Polish composer Frédéric Chopin, for he has been chosen to be the namesake for his immense dedication to the piano, his revolutionary change of the piano technique and inspiration for millions. Chopin has seldom composed for instruments other than the piano, his dedication to the instrument gave him the nickname the "Poet of the Piano".
